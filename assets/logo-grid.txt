LEGEND
================================================================================

  Concept 1 — Copilot Bird + Orchestration Rings
    The Copilot bird is wrapped in orbital rings (like electron shells),
    each containing an agent dot — representing agents orbiting the core
    Copilot engine. Symmetric, technical, evocative of both the Copilot
    brand and the multi-agent layer OMP adds on top.

  Concept 2 — Terminal + Agent Network
    A CLI-first aesthetic: prompt prefix, node-graph topology. Agents
    form a connected mesh with the orchestrator at center. Shows
    interconnection without sacrificing the developer-tool feel.

  Concept 3 — Plug-and-Play / Extension
    Literalizes OMP as a plugin slot with pins and a module body.
    Communicates "extension of Copilot CLI" immediately. Bold block
    characters and connector lines reinforce the physical metaphor.

  Concept 4 — Rocket / Fast Workflow
    ASCII rocket with exhaust trail spelling "LAUNCH SEQUENCE".
    Represents OMP's mission: fast, automated, launch-ready workflows.
    High energy, single-focus, developer-heroic tone.

  Concept 5 — Brain / Agents Thinking Together
    A neural/organic brain form built from OMP's letters. The three
    letters interlock like synaptic connections. Represents the
    collective intelligence of 18 specialized agents working in sync.

  Concept 6 — GitHub Octocat Fusion
    The Octocat head merged with an OMP shield. The antenna doubles as
    the OMP lightning bolt. Inherits GitHub trust while signaling OMP's
    unique identity as an extension layer.

  Concept 7 — Orchestra Conductor
    Musical staff lines, conductor's baton, and the OMP letters arranged
    like a score. Agents are instruments; OMP is the conductor bringing
    them into harmony. Playful, human-centric, memorable.

  Concept 8 — Constellation / Stars
    Agents as stars forming an orbital constellation around the OMP core.
    Scattered star density gives depth; the center is stable. Represents
    scale, reach, and the distributed nature of a multi-agent system.

  Concept 9 — Minimalist OMP Monogram
    Strict block-character rendering of OMP. No decoration, pure form.
    Works at any scale. The ASCII-block letterforms have a terminal-native
    feel that fits a CLI plugin while being instantly legible and bold.
